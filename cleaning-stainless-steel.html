Cleaning the outside of stainless steel appliances

Clean the outside of stainless steel appliances, such as the refrigerator, range, dishwasher, and microwave oven.

To complete this task, you need the following supplies:
  • Stainless steel wipes, such as Weiman Stainless Steel Wipes
  • Microfiber cloth

Complete the following steps:

  1. Using a stainless steel wipe, wipe from left-to-right or right-to-left in a horizontal motion.
  2. With the cloth, use the same horizontal motion to remove cleaning residue and shine the surface.
  3. Use a fresh cloth to clean the handles of the appliances.
